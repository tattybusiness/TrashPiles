Our core team comes from a very outdoorsy background. We are avid fishermen who have spent countless hours on the water. The amount of trash and pollution that we see is both saddening and alarming at the same time.

We truly wanted to develop a project that was both fun and could benefit our planet. In doing so, we have decided to make a sizeable donation to a charity that is going to make a true difference in the world. We searched far and wide for a reputable charity to donate funds to once raised and are hoping to have a partnership prior to our minting date!
